Product Saver

In an effort to reuse plastic our company removes from the ocean, we decided to create a product that helps reduce food waste.

  • The Safest Materials

  • Made from recycled plastic

    Reduces plastic sitting in landfills and possibly ending up in our oceans.

  • Food-grade/ BPA

    Safe for food, safe for the environment. Uses BPA free plastic.

  • Dishwasher & Microwave Safe

  • Easy to Use

  • Freshness-lock lid

    Snap lock lids lock in freshness.

  • Lifted Basket

    Basket is fitted to allow air to travel between the sides and bottom of the container, and keeps produce from sitting at the bottom with excess water.

  • Multi-Use

    Store fruits and vegetables but also acts as a colander when washing to help let out excess water.

FAQ

  • Can I purchase this product online?

    While online shopping is in the works, our produce saver can only be found in Target, Whole Foods, and Hannaford Stores at the moment.

  • How should I wash this product?

    The produce saver is top shelf dishwasher safe, but can also be washed by hand with warm soapy water.

  • Is using recycled plastic safe?

    Our products are made using sanitized, recycled plastic. We only use plastics that are food safe and free of harmful chemicals. Our products are tested by our scientists before heading out to ensure they are high quality and safe.
